Projects

Explore case studies that demonstrate loss reduction, efficiency gains, and repeatable operations.

MixIt - Concretti Designs

Custom, internal tool software for a luxury concrete production company that included: - a recipe calculator array for batch mixing [saved them 6 hours a week in labor put into making calculations manually] -a job volume calculator that let's the user know the total volume of material needed to finish the job -a yardage calculator for ready-mix orders -Mix Design & Color Design Managers that save your mix recipes

Scorpius Project - Valley Precast

Moble Vault made to contain various types of rays, radiation, and nuclear material. Made for the National Nuclear Security Administration. I was project manager, overseeing production, quality control, and coordinating with 12 individual entities, both private, and public. Project was completed ahead of schedule, within budget, and without snags.

90° Project - Copart

Re-grading yards for Copart to increase usable inventory space by restructuring layout. Using smaller transport machines allowed narrower drive lanes and a shift from 45° to 90° vehicle orientation, boosting yard capacity by ~42% and increasing real estate value. I also developed a measurement cheat sheet that more than doubled team efficiency while eliminating the need for costly surveying lasers, reducing overall downtime.
