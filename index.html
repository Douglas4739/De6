Clear milestones so you know exactly what to expect at every stage.

  1. Define- Audience + Goals + Existing Process Audit
  2. Measure- Collect Data + Validate Measurement System + Establish Baselines

Scorpius Project - Valley Precast

Moble Vault made to contain various types of rays, radiation, and nuclear material. Made for the National Nuclear Security Administration. I was project manager, overseeing production, quality control, and coordinating with 12 individual entities, both private, and public. View Project Gallery

90° Project - Copart

Re-grading yards for Copart to increase usable inventory space by restructuring layout. Using smaller transport machines allowed narrower drive lanes and a shift from 45° to 90° vehicle orientation, boosting yard capacity by ~42% and increasing real estate value. I also developed a measurement cheat sheet that more than doubled team efficiency while eliminating the need for costly surveying lasers, reducing overall downtime.
